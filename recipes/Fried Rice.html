Chahan Egg Fried Rice

Description

"Chahan Japanese Fried Rice is a captivating anime-inspired culinary journey. With a perfect blend of traditional Japanese flavors and a playful touch, each bite tells a flavorful tale of creativity. Immerse yourself in the magic of Chahan, where anime meets expertly crafted fried rice for a delightful dining experience."

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 40 g Char Siu Chinese-flavored barbecued pork
  • 2 tbsp Long Green Onions (Naganegi) chopped
  • 1 Garlic Clove chopped
  • 2 tsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Sake
  • 150 g Steamed Rice
  • 20 g Spring Onion Leaves chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Soy Sauce to taste (or separately)
