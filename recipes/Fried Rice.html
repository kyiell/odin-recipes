Chahan Egg Fried Rice

Description

"Chahan Japanese Fried Rice is a captivating anime-inspired culinary journey. With a perfect blend of traditional Japanese flavors and a playful touch, each bite tells a flavorful tale of creativity. Immerse yourself in the magic of Chahan, where anime meets expertly crafted fried rice for a delightful dining experience."

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 40 g Char Siu Chinese-flavored barbecued pork
  • 2 tbsp Long Green Onions (Naganegi) chopped
  • 1 Garlic Clove chopped
  • 2 tsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Sake
  • 150 g Steamed Rice
  • 20 g Spring Onion Leaves chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Soy Sauce to taste (or separately)

Instructions

  1. Cut the char siu, Chinese-flavored barbecued pork into small cubes.
  2. Beat your egg in a small bowl.
  3. Turn on your burner and add your vegetable oil to a pan. Add your chopped naganegi and stir-fry on low heat.
  4. Add in your chopped garlic clove and increase heat a bit.
  5. Add the beaten egg and hot steamed rice.
  6. Add the beaten egg and hot steamed rice.
  7. Top with your char siu and stir.
  8. Add your salt and pepper to taste and mix.
  9. Top with chopped spring onion leave and mix some more.
  10. Add soy sauce or serve and then top with soy sauce individually.
